About Us

Get to know the team behind Stantechs Solutions

Our Story

Stantechs Solutions was founded with a simple mission: to help businesses thrive in the digital age. Based in Eldoret, Kenya, we started as a small team of passionate developers and designers who believed that every business deserves access to quality tech solutions.

Today, we've grown into a full-service technology company serving clients across Kenya and beyond. From web development to cybersecurity, we provide comprehensive solutions tailored to meet the unique needs of each client.

Our Mission

To empower businesses with innovative technology solutions that drive growth, efficiency, and success in the digital landscape.

Our Vision

To be the leading technology partner for businesses in Africa, known for our excellence, innovation, and commitment to client success.

Our Values

Integrity, Innovation, Excellence, Client-Focus, and Continuous Learning guide everything we do at Stantechs Solutions.
